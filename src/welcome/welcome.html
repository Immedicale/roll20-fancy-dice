Thank you for installing Fancy Dice for Roll20

To use the extension, join a Roll20 game and click on the extension's icon (the blue d20) in your browser. A pop-up will open, where you can change your settings and choose your dice.

Every once in a while Roll20 updates their code, which can sometimes break the extension. If your 3D dice suddenly won't load, this is likely the cause. Please send me a message at tobloef@gmail.com or open an issue on GitHub if this happens and I will try to update the extension as soon as possible.

If you want to get involved with adding new dice to the extension, please also feel free to get in touch, I would love to help you get started.

I hope you enjoy the extension and have fun playing!
